Average no. of particles dissociated from 1 molecule.
Common van't hoff factors are- 
1. **Non-Electrolyte**: non-dissociating $\to$ $i = 1$.
2. **Strong-Electrolyte**: 100% Ionization ($\alpha = 1$) 
$$
i = 1-\alpha + n\alpha
$$
$$
i= n
$$
3. **Weak-Electrolyte**: ($\alpha<1$)
$$
i = 1-\alpha+n\alpha
$$
