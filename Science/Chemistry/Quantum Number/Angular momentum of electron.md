The formula to find Angular momentum of electron is,
$$
L_{e^-} = \frac{nh}{2\pi}
$$
Where,
- $h$ = plank's constant.
- $n$ is the principal quantum number of the electron's orbit

>This Concept of electron in Bohr's orbit, violates the Heisenberg uncertainty principle.
