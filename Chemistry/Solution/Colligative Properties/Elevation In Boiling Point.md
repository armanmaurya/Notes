$$
\boxed{\Delta T_{b} = i.k_{b} \times m}
$$
Where, $K_{b} = \cfrac{RT_{b}^2}{1000 \times l_{v}}$ , $l_{v} = \cfrac{\Delta H_{\text{vap}}}{M}$ $

Where, 
- $T_{b}$ = B.P of pure solvent
- $l_{v}$ = Latent heat of vapourization per gm
- $K_{b}$ molar elevation constant 
- $M$ = molar mass
